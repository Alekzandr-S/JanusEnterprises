Project Overview

A multi‑site supply engagement covering angle grinders, demolition hammers, cordless drills, welding gear, safety PPE, and consumables. Janus managed vendor selection, price benchmarking, import logistics, and last‑mile delivery to sites across Zambia.

Procurement & Vendor Management

Competitive sourcing with brand options (DeWalt, Makita, Bosch, Ingco) to meet varying budgets.

Logistics & Delivery

Coordinated imports and regional distribution with tracked deliveries and proof of receipt.

After‑sales Support

Warranty claims, repairs, and replacements handled quickly to minimize downtime.

Scope & Deliverables

Supply Categories

Angle grinders, rotary hammers, impact drivers, drills, welders, compressors, generators, PPE (helmets, gloves, boots), abrasives & cutting discs.

Service Inclusions

Quotations & pro‑formas, brand alternatives, technical spec matching, delivery scheduling, on‑site handover.

Quality & Compliance

Genuine parts, manufacturer warranty handling, safety standards adherence, batch documentation.
