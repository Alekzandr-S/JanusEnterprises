About Us

Janus Enterprises Limited is a Zambian company supplying quality products and delivering professional installations and services for industry, construction, retail, and households. We focus on speed, reliability, and long-term partnerships, from sourcing and procurement to delivery, install, and after-sales support.

Our portfolio spans industrial & power tools, electrical and plumbing, kitchen fittings & shop displays, security & lighting, and general hardware. We back this up with repairs, site work, and project coordination so you get one accountable partner end-to-end.

What we do

  • Product sourcing & supply (tools, fittings, hardware)
  • Installations: electrical, plumbing, security, lighting
  • Kitchen fittings & shop display accessories
  • Repairs, servicing, and after-sales support
  • Logistics coordination & site deliveries
  • Project quotations & pro-forma invoices

Sectors we serve

  • Mining & industrial operations
  • Construction & property development
  • Retail, hospitality & commercial spaces
  • Residential & estate projects
  • Agriculture & community infrastructure

How we work

  • Share requirements → we advise on options & availability
  • Site visit / measurements where needed
  • Quotation or pro-forma, then sourcing & delivery
  • Installation & handover, with ongoing support
